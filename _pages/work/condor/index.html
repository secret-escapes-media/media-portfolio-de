Introduction

Grenada is one of the lesser-known islands of the Caribbean. We wanted to showcase that Grenada is a real hidden gem. With a direct route between Germany and Grenada by Condor, the challenge was to show our members that Grenada is the island escape they have been waiting for - and is, of course, extremely accessible.

The Solution

We created a landing page where we highlighted Grenada as a very special Caribbean destination, promoting its ‘hidden gem’ qualities and, importantly, showcasing the ease of booking a break with Secret Escapes and Condor. With special flight offers and incredible Secret Escapes packages, members were not only inspired, but also shown a tangible way of having their Caribbean dream realised. This was our third campaign with Grenada and the uptake in engagement and bookings shows that it is striking a chord with our members.
